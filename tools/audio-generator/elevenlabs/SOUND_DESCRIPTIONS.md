## Sound Design Notes

### Duration Guidelines
- Weapon shots: 0.1-0.35s (snappy feedback)
- Impacts: 0.1-0.25s (quick response)
- Small explosions: 0.4-0.8s
- Large explosions: 0.9-1.5s
- Boss death: 7.5s (epic sequence)
- UI sounds: 0.05-0.6s (responsive)
- Loopable ambient: 1.0-3.0s

### Style Guidance
- Modern cinematic sci-fi (not 8-bit/retro)
- Punchy weapon sounds with clear attack transients
- Rich bass in explosions and engine sounds
- Clean, responsive UI feedback
- Organic/biological for Swarm faction
- Ethereal/mysterious for Void faction
- Aggressive/harsh for Pirate faction
- Industrial/mechanical for Rogue Miner faction
- Unstable/erratic for Scavenger faction

### Loopable Sounds
Mark sounds that need seamless looping:
- Engine thrust (all tiers)
- Star proximity (all sizes)
- Wormhole ambient
- Space ambient
